# Activities and Recreation
We offer a variety of programs and activities for everyone. Whether you are interested in special programs, affordable fun, sports, arts, fitness, health, wellness, or community improvement, we have something to offer you. Join us and enjoy the many activities and programs available in our community!
Our [Access Without Fear policy](https://www.cityofkingston.ca/council-and-city-administration/indigenization-inclusion-diversity-equity-and-accessibility-iidea/#access) means everyone has access to City services, no matter their immigration status. We will not report a person's immigration status to Canada Border Services or Police.
